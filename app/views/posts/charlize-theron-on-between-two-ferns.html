There is a new "Between Two Ferns" with Zach Galifianakis over at FunnyOrDie.com - this time with Charlize Theron. With most of the previous 'Two Fern' interview series, Zach does a remarkable job of combining an awkward, crass, and clueless approach to (supposedly) piss off his interviewee, and typically in short order. Prior interviewees include: Michael Cera, Jimmy Kimmel, Jon Hamm, Bradley Cooper, and Natalie Portman.

In this interview, the tables seem a bit turned; his approach has a different effect.

Never has an advertisement for Need for Speed: Shift been so convincing.
